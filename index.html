Our Senior Pastor

Apostle Emmanuel Nsoh

Born in Kumasi, Ghana, Pastor Emmanuel received his call to ministry in 1996 and founded The Word Ministries International in Zorkor in 2013. His anointed teaching ministry has led to church planting and the spiritual transformation of hundreds of thousands of lives.

"The Word of God is not just a book — it is a living, breathing encounter with the Creator of the universe. My mission is simple: help people meet God through His Word."

— Pastor Emmanuel Nsoh

A graduate of the Electrical Technician Training Institute of bolgatanga, Ghana and the Asobono Bible Training Centre, Pastor Emmanuel also holds a Bible coodinator roleat ETM . He is married to Madam. Elizabith Nsoh, co-founder and Women's Ministry Director. Together they have three children and No grandchildren.
