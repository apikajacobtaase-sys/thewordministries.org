A Prayer Declaration from Pastor Emma


Beloved in Christ, I declare that the grace, peace, and favor of God will rest upon your life. May the Lord strengthen you in every season, guide your steps, and open doors of opportunity that no one can shut. I pray that every burden weighing on your heart be lifted, every fear be replaced with faith, and every challenge become a testimony of God's goodness. May divine wisdom direct your decisions, divine protection surround your family, and divine provision meet every need according to God's riches in glory. I declare healing for the sick, restoration for the brokenhearted, strength for the weary, and hope for those who feel discouraged. May the light of Christ shine upon you, and may His love fill your heart with joy and peace. In the mighty name of Jesus Christ, may you walk in victory, purpose, and abundant blessings today and always. Amen. — Pastor Emma

Our Senior Pastor

Apostle Emmanuel Nsoh

"The Word of God is not just a book — it is a living, breathing encounter with the Creator of the universe. My mission is simple: help people meet God through His Word."

— Apostle Emmanuel Nsoh

Pastor Emma is a passionate and dedicated servant of God committed to preaching the Gospel, teaching biblical truth, and helping people grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ. Through inspiring messages, spiritual guidance, and community outreach, Pastor Emma seeks to impact lives, strengthen faith, and encourage individuals to live according to God's purpose. Known for compassion, integrity, and a heart for ministry, Pastor Emma continues to serve as a source of encouragement and spiritual leadership to many.
